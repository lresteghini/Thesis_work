These files are the pieces of code I used to conduct the numerical simulations related to my bachelor's thesis under Prof. Dario Tamascelli and Dott. Davide Ferracin.

They relate to the simulation of a simple quantum excitonic transport network coupled to resonant modes, with the objective of observing and highlighting the advantage a resonant harmonic oscillator coupled to each side provides to the efficiency of the energy transfer process.

Some useful sources:

"Enhancing light-harvesting power with coherent vibrational interactions: A quantum heat engine picture" (N. Killoran et al 2015)
"Noise-assisted energy transfer in quantum networks and light-harvesting complexes" (A.W. Chin et al 2010)
"Efficient energy transfer in light-harvesting systems, I: optimal temperature, reorganization energy and spatial–temporal correlations" (JianlanWu et al 2010)
